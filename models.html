📘 Understanding Linear Regression Models

Each organism has its own trained linear regression model predicting abundance from 4 water quality parameters. Trained on 15 samples with complete environmental data (Samples 1–8 and 16–23). R² measures model fit (0–1, higher is better). RMSE is average prediction error (lower is better).
